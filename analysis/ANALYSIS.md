# Website Data Analysis

## Dataset Overview
**68 top websites** from 2018-2019 rankings (Alexa & SimilarWeb)

---

## Key Findings

### Geographic Distribution
- **U.S. dominates** with 32 websites (46.4%)
- **China** follows with 15 websites (21.7%) 
- **Russia** third with 5 websites (7.2%)
- Only **15 countries** represented globally

### Most Common Website Types
1. **Internet services and products** - 13 websites
2. **Portal sites** - 6 websites
3. **Social networks** - 4 websites
4. **Search engines** - 4 websites
5. **Online shopping** - 4 websites

### Dominant Business Models
- **Internet services and products**: Most successful (13 sites)
- **E-commerce models**: Strong presence (6 sites combined)
- **Advertising-supported platforms**: Highly successful
- **Multi-service portals**: Consistent performers

### Why This Historical Data Matters
- **Benchmark** for understanding internet evolution
- **Core players** (Google, Facebook, Amazon) remain dominant
- **Geographic patterns** (U.S./China dominance) continue today
- **Business model validation** - shows sustainable approaches
- **Academic value** for studying internet landscape changes
